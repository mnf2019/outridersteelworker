Our Story

Forged From Experience

Founded by a craftsman with decades of hands-on expertise, Outrider has grown from a small Spruce Grove workshop into one of Alberta's most trusted steel fabrication operations.

1987

Industry Roots

Surj Purewal begins his career in the steel fabrication industry, spending years mastering every facet of the trade.

2003

Outrider Founded

After 16 years of industry experience, Surj opens Outrider Steelworks Ltd. in a small shop in Spruce Grove, Alberta.

2004

First Expansion

Rapid growth drives a move to a larger facility in Acheson, Alberta — within just one year of founding.

2008

Current Home

Outrider relocates to its permanent facility at 978 Boulder Boulevard in Stony Plain's North Business Park.

Now

Continued Growth

We continue to provide highly skilled services and quality fabricated products to clients across Alberta and Canada.

SP

Surj Purewal

Founder & President

With over three decades of hands-on experience in the steel fabrication industry, Surj built Outrider on the principles of quality craftsmanship, precision engineering, and reliable delivery. His deep expertise in bridge construction components defines the company's uncompromising standards.
